Welcome to Libido-Links. Your starting point for all things Pedo on the Dark Web.

Rules

For a site to be listed it must conform to the following rules. Please note that these rules are evolving and may be subject to change.

  1. Sites must be public, not private. If accounts are required, the registration process must be easily available, simple and clear. Invite-only sites are private and will not be listed.
  2. Sites must not require any personal info to create an account.
  3. Sites must work with TBB in "safest" mode (no JS).
  4. Sites must not require payment for any part of site.
  5. Sites must not exclusively cater to hurtcore.
  6. Sites must be on-topic.
